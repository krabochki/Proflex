What is Proflex?

This is a website for learning professional vocabulary in English and the English language in general. Materials on various topics for different specialties are collected and structured here. Professional vocabulary is one of the components of successful employees in various fields of activity. Whether you like it or not, career growth and success are impossible without constant development and self-improvement. For specialists of all ages, the question of studying professional vocabulary in a foreign language is increasingly being raised for various reasons. First of all - for their own convenience.

For what do you need to learn English?

It won't be an exaggeration to say that English is the most important language in the world. Nearly half of the world population speaks English. Some of the largest countries in the world are English-speaking. Among them the USA, Canada, Australia and Great Britain.

English has become an international language due to its significance in many areas, such as industry, business, finance, agriculture, and else.

It’s the accepted language of modern digital era. A lot of problems of the 21st century, such as the problems of war and peace, ecology, can not be solved without speaking the same language.
